What does end of support for Community Edition mean for users?

As of February, 4th, 2019, Wakanda Community Edition will no longer be available for download on our website. The users will no longer be eligible for new releases or support. Only the Enterprise Edition will be available for download and support.

Will there be any new releases and upgrades in Wakanda platform?

Yes! The Enterprise Edition is still here for the long haul. Each new release will be available for download. We offer an opportunity for a 30 days free trial to test it out.

Will existing releases for Wakanda Community Edition continue to be available?

Old releases are still available on Github but there will be no new updates.

How do I get Wakanda Enterprise Edition?

You can download the 30 days free trial on our download page. Contact us to know more about how we can help you make the best of it.

Has support ended for all users already using the Community Edition?

Yes. With the Community out of support and upgrades, we recommend that customers move to the Enterprise Edition.
